The Tao Process

1. Dream

We encourage self inspiration with entrepreneurs and stakeholders to find the inner space to find the vision they seek. We provide tools, patience, and atmospheres that promote dreams to manifest.

2. Discover

When ready, we conduct a full Discovery Analysis to cover an enterprise level strategy. This discovery process can be quick or in-depth, depending on the project at hand. We use Big Data & UX/UI Research to continuously iterate mind maps,solution architecture plans, and ERPs until all parties are satisfied with the plan to move forward.

3. Focus

Our Cloud Productivity team and Agile Coaches use the plans we design to start creating formal policies, procedures, and training task forces to accomplish goals. We implement solutions through Focus Hives with teams where we track small iterations of progress through OKRs, KPIs, and a variety of our own soft skill analytics known as Curiosity Scores.
