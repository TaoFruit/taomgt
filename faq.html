What is Solution Architecture?
Solution Architecture is a practice of defining and describing an architecture system delivered in context of a specific solution. Solution Architecture may encompass the description of an entire system or only its specific parts. Solution Architects are assigned to concrete projects to ensure the technical integrity and consistency of each solution at certain stages during the solutions lifecycle.

Is there a Refund Policy?
Your initial payment is non-refundable. This is due to the time we set aside for you upon being hired. After the hiring process we may turned away other opportunities in order to provide you with personalized quality assurance. Subsequent payments are subject to a pro-rated refund if you choose to terminate the project before we have fully earned the projects funding. For example, suppose fees for your project are broken into three payments, each covering 20 hours of services. The initial payment covers the first 20 hours, and the second payment covers the next 20 hours. If you change your mind about the project after we have put a total of 30 hours into the project, you would be entitled to a refund for half of the second payment because we will only have earned 10 hours of your projects allotted time.
